About Rapid Auto Repair

Cambridge's trusted automotive repair experts since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Rapid Auto Repair has been serving the Cambridge community with honest, reliable automotive repair services for over 15 years. What started as a small family-owned shop has grown into Cambridge's most trusted auto repair destination.

Our mission is simple: provide exceptional automotive repair services with transparency, integrity, and expertise. We believe every customer deserves honest pricing, quality workmanship, and vehicles that run safely and efficiently.

Located on Franklin Boulevard, we've built our reputation on treating every customer like family and every vehicle like our own. Our team of certified mechanics brings decades of combined experience to every repair, from routine maintenance to complex engine rebuilds.
