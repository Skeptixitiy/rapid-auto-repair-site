Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our automotive repair services

How long do most repairs take?

Most routine services like oil changes take 30-60 minutes. More complex repairs may take 1-3 days depending on parts availability. We provide time estimates upfront and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes! We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. Parts and labor are typically covered for 12 months or 12,000 miles, whichever comes first. Specific warranty terms vary by service type.

Can you work on all vehicle makes and models?

Absolutely! Our certified mechanics have experience with all major automotive brands including domestic, European, and Asian vehicles. We use manufacturer-approved parts and follow OEM specifications for all repairs.
